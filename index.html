00 / About

Business developer by person. Creative director by practice.

For 15 years I have built relations, products and teams at the intersection of Scandinavian aesthetics and the commercial mechanics of international retail.

Today I direct Says Who — a senior product development consultancy that has been a driving force in the furniture industry, developing product programmes for some of the world's leading brands and retailers.

My work sits where commercial strategy meets engineering reality: stage-gate pipelines, material systems, licensing models, and — increasingly — how AI and internal tooling reshape the way a consultancy develops, engineers and delivers product.
